About AgriConnect Kenya

Empowering Farmers, Connecting Markets

Our Mission

To revolutionize agricultural trade in Kenya by providing small-scale farmers with direct access to market information and buyers, ensuring fair prices and sustainable farming practices.

Our Story

AgriConnect Kenya was born from a simple observation: small-scale farmers often struggle to get fair prices for their produce due to limited market access and information. We started in 2024 with a vision to bridge this gap using technology.

Today, we serve thousands of farmers across Kenya, helping them make informed decisions and connect directly with buyers, eliminating unnecessary intermediaries and ensuring better returns for their hard work.

Our Values

Integrity

We believe in transparent, honest relationships with our farmers and buyers.

Community

Building strong, supportive farming communities across Kenya.

Sustainability

Promoting sustainable farming practices for future generations.

Innovation

Using technology to solve traditional farming challenges.
