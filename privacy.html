Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2024

1. Information We Collect

At AgriConnect Kenya, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number)
  • Farm location and produce details
  • Market preferences and trading history
  • Device information and usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Facilitating connections between farmers and buyers
  • Sending price alerts and market updates
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing fraud

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • Verified buyers (only when you choose to connect)
  • Service providers who assist our operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement various security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
